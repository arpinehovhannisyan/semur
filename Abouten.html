"Semur & Co" LLC company is one of the largest companies in the construction materials industry in Armenia. It was founded in 2003 by Virabyan's family.
"Semur & Co" company offers consumers galvanized, pre-painted galvanized roofing with wide choice of colors, metal tiles and roofing accessories.
The company was the first in the RA to start the production of corrugated sheets, metal tiles, roofing accessories, gypsum profiles and beacons, various kinds of meshes, as well as nails and buckets.
At the same time "Semur & Co" is engaged in import and sale of building materials.
The product range is imported from different countries of the world, particularly from South Korea, China, Iran.
    The main goals of our company are:
  • •providing local market with high quality products
  • •the provision of high-quality construction services
  • •easy access to sales centers
  • •saving time and additional resources
